My website is a shifting house next to a river of knowledge. What could yours be?

By Beatrice Warde

For me, the website is like a box.

If you put in too many items, it's obviously heavy, takes time to load.

If you made it too flashy, it's obviously hard to absorb the information.

If you do not make the handle, the functional is obviously not satisfied.

So

Keep my website clean, functional and attractive is what I orientation to!
